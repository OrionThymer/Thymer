Saute Onions
3 min

Add 1 of coconut oil into a large sauce pan and heat at on a medium heat. Once the oil is hot, start to saute the onions for five minutes or until they start to brown.

You should take this time to checko on your rice.
Add Rice and Stir
200 min

Once the rice and onions are ready, mix the rice onto the sauce pan and start stirring it with the sauted onions. Do this for five minutes while adding in old bay.

If you don't think you need more old bay, you need more old bay.
Scramble Egg
4 min

Once you've added enough old bay and your rice is looking sufficiently seasoned, crack two eggs and scramble them into your stir fry. Keep mixing the dish until the eggs are cooked.

If your stir fry is looking ready earlier, turn down the heat and serve.
